How to play
The basics
  1. Spin & draft — You make 6 picks. For each one you spin a random era + club, then choose any player from that club — they drop into their position automatically. Your squad is a flexible front five (3 Guards + 1 Forward + 1 Center, or 2 Guards + 2 Forwards + 1 Center) plus a Wildcard (anyone). Don’t like a club? You get one re-spin per draft. Tap two spots to swap.
  2. Win categories — Squads go head-to-head on 6 stats: Points, Rebounds, Assists, Steals, Blocks and Turnovers (fewer wins). Win the most of the 6 to take the match — 1 point per category. A 3–3 tie is decided by your clutch shooter (below).
  3. Clutch shooter — When your squad is full, tap your buzzer-beater (their shooting % stays hidden from opponents). If a match ends level, they take the last shot — the higher their eFG%, the better your chances of winning.
Three ways to play
🏆
Challenge Your FriendsA private online tournament. Create it, share the code, and everyone drafts. Once all players have joined and drafted it plays a round-robin — best record is champion. No-shows? Start with who’s in or drop them, then anyone kicks off the next round. Best for a small group, head-to-head over rounds.
🌎
Challenge the WorldOne global, all-time ladder. Submit a squad and it’s compared against everyone for a W–L record and a rank. Played in monthly seasons: whoever’s top when the month ends is crowned champion, then the board resets. Best for one squad vs. the whole world.
🏅
LeaguesYour own mini-world — public or private. Create or join one, then draft as many teams as you like (up to 10). They all compete on the league’s own all-time ladder. Best for an ongoing competition with a group you choose.
In short: Friends is a structured tournament with people you invite · the World is one giant monthly leaderboard · Leagues are your own ongoing mini-Worlds.

Welcome to Euroleague Guru
Draft an all-time EuroLeague dream team and prove you know the game better than anyone.
How the draft works
1
Spin for every pickYour squad needs 6 players. For each one you spin the wheel — it lands on a random era + club, and you draft from whatever comes up. Got a club you don’t like? You get one re-spin per draft.
2
Pick anyone — they slot inThe club’s whole roster opens up with every player’s stats, so you can weigh your options. Tap a player and they drop into the right spot for you: 2 Guards, a Forward, a Center, a flex Guard/Forward, and a Wildcard (any position). Tap two spots to swap them around.
3
Then competeOnce your 6 are in, pick your clutch shooter (for tie-breaks) and face rivals on 6 stats — points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks and fewest turnovers. Win the most categories to win the match, vs friends, the world, or your own league.
